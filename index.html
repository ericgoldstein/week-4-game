Crystal Collector

This is how you play the game!


  1. You will be given a random Total to get at the start of the game."The Number To Get"
  2. There are 4 crystals to chose below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score for the game.
  4. You can win the game by matching your total score to the "Number to Get".
  5. You will lose the game if your total amount of points goes over the "Number to get".
  6. The value of each crystal is unknown until you click it.
  7. Every time the game starts the game will assign new values to each crystal.
